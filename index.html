Still loving you

At times, I yearn for your presence beside me. While I do not miss you when I am alone, I miss you constantly, from work to family gatherings, your presence constantly occupies my mind. There are even moments when I used to leave early to call you, eagerly awaiting your response. I am not deterred by the perception of being labeled as eccentric for loving someone who resides miles away; my love for you stems solely from the fact that you exist.

Since our initial contact, every aspect of my life has undergone a profound transformation. My initial intention was not to cause you any inconvenience, but even as our relationship progressed, my reliance on my phone increased to an alarming extent. Even now, when you have explicitly expressed your desire for no further contact, I find myself unable to control my hands from reaching for my phone. I am filled with uncertainty and confusion, unable to comprehend the events that have transpired. I had anticipated a closer connection with you, but ultimately, I will only be remembered as a deranged individual, consumed by an obsessive desire to see you once before succumbing to madness.

I would like to request clarification, as our love is enduring. Aspen, I am certain that we are destined to be together, sharing both simple and extravagant moments. While you may perceive it as a vague promise, since my birth, every aspiration I have for myself has come to fruition, demonstrating that my will surpasses your comprehension. You are, in essence, my ideal Aspen.

I am perplexed by your departure, but I am more concerned about your well-being. I had promised you that my life’s purpose was to meet you in person at least once.

Do you recall the tale of an individual consumed by an intense obsession with the woman he held dear? His life had become a dreary existence, consumed solely by her thoughts. Determined to fulfill his destiny, he abandoned everything to seek her out.

At the place where she resided, he approached her, his heart pounding with anticipation. She was gracefully displaying her beauty to the world. Suddenly, her gaze met that of a despondent man standing on the street, surrounded by discarded debris. He was captivated by her presence, tears streaming down his face. She, oblivious to his presence, seemed to have forgotten everything.

In an instant, he found the courage to open his heart and reveal his true feelings to her. With a heavy heart, he uttered, “I promised to see you again, my love." I will remain the same person you know. Tears welled up in his eyes as he made his heartfelt declaration...

Overwhelmed with a profound sense of self-loathing, I find myself utterly devoid of any purpose in life.

I love you Aspen.

—A.
